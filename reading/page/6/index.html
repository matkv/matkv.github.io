2021

Rhythm of War (The Stormlight Archive, #4)
Brandon Sanderson (2020)
★★★★★

Personally I didn’t love that most of the book to place in the same location but overall it was amazing as usual. I actually didn’t mind the pacing and the finale was great and left me eager to read the next one once it’s out!

Finished: 24 March 2021


Dawnshard (The Stormlight Archive, #3.5)
Brandon Sanderson (2020)
★★★★☆

I really like the novellas in between the long books of The Stormlight Archive. Dawnshard was a nice change of pace and gave us a chance to spend more time with Rysn. Sandersons writing style was nice as usual and didn’t get in the way of following the story. Overall I liked the book!

Finished: 21 January 2021


1984
George Orwell (1949)
★★★★☆

I personally don’t find Orwell’s writing still to be very engaging but overall I liked the book, definitely worth reading it for the references and general impact on popular culture.

Finished: 14 January 2021


2020

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows (Harry Potter, #7)
J.K. Rowling (2007)
★★★★☆

Overall a nice end to the whole series, some of the conclusions/explanations to some plot holes felt kind of weak but again, the book suceeds at what it is trying to be.

Finished: 19 December 2020


Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (Harry Potter, #6)
J.K. Rowling (2005)
★★★★☆

Probably my favorite Harry Potter book. I think the general atmosphere in this one is the best. We still get cozy scenes at Hogwarts but the story has by now definitely shifted to a darker tone and there is a sense of urgency and danger. Liked it a lot!

Finished: 8 December 2020
