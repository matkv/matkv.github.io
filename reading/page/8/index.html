Animal Farm
George Orwell (1945)
★★☆☆☆

I get the overall message, but I just found it way too predictable and just like with 1984 I just don’t really love Orwell’s writing style. All in all I think it’s worth the read though, even if it’s just for the references in popular culture and general idea and political allegory.

If it hadn’t been such a super short read I would have probably quit reading it.

Finished: 18 April 2020


Oathbringer (The Stormlight Archive, #3)
Brandon Sanderson (2017)
★★★★★

Fantastic just like the other ones in the series. I actually didn’t have problems with the pacing like some other reviewers had, overall I loved it. Great characters, a page turner. Would recommend!

Finished: 16 April 2020


Edgedancer (The Stormlight Archive, #2.5)
Brandon Sanderson (2017)
★★★★☆

Almost skipped this one because it’s technically optional to read this novella when looking at the whole series. Glad I didn’t though, really enjoyable read even though it might not be as “big” as the other books.

Finished: 17 March 2020


Words of Radiance (The Stormlight Archive, #2)
Brandon Sanderson (2014)
★★★★★

Just as amazing as the first one of the series. Amazing worldbuilding and characters, super immersive story. Fantastic book!

Finished: 14 March 2020


The Way of Kings (The Stormlight Archive, #1)
Brandon Sanderson (2010)
★★★★★

Absolutely loved it. Incredible worldbuilding, amazing characters and story - an absolute page turner. I genuinely can’t think of a lot of things that I disliked about the book.

Finished: 10 November 2019
